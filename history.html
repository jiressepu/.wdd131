Our Story

CréaWeb Academy was born out of the passion of industry experts determined to make digital skills accessible to everyone. Founded in 2015, we’ve been training thousands of students ever since.

Over the years, we have helped our learners acquire key skills and launch new careers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality training that is accessible and aligned with job market needs.

We emphasize hands-on learning to ensure the employability of our students.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Open education for all, without barriers.
  • Practical Learning: Training focused on real-world projects.
  • Innovation: Continuous monitoring for up-to-date training.
  • Community: A network of support and sharing.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Personalized mentorship and guidance toward employment.
  • Courses designed with industry experts.
  • Real-world projects for hands-on experience.
